ABOUT SHIKSHA SAMRIDDHI : OVERVIEW

Mission: Improve the educational landscape in India by standardizing school structures to align with the Samagra Shiksha framework and UDISE+ guidelines.

Objective: Address challenges posed by odd school structures by providing AI-driven tools and integrating with UDISE+ for effective school restructuring.

Approach: Utilize advanced AI technology for analysis and classification of school structures, and offer actionable recommendations for alignment with national standards.

Features:

  • AI-driven school structure analysis.
  • Integration with UDISE+ for data synchronization.
  • Customizable dashboards for real-time monitoring.
  • Scenario planning tools for restructuring simulations.
  • Community feedback and engagement portal.

Target: Schools across India with non-standard configurations, particularly in regions with high percentages of odd structures.

Goal: Facilitate the transition from odd to standard school structures, ensuring uniformity in educational delivery and equitable resource distribution.
