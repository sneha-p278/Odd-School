Brief Overview

Across India, many schools are categorized as having 'odd structures.' These are schools that, due to historical, geographical, or administrative reasons, do not follow the standard grade configurations or lack necessary facilities. This leads to challenges in resource allocation, curriculum implementation, and ultimately, student outcomes. Aligning these schools with the Samagra Shiksha framework is essential to ensure every child receives a high-quality education, regardless of where they live.
